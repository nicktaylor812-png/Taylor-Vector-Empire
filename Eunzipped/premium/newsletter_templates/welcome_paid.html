Understanding Your Reports

Edge %: The statistical advantage we've identified. Higher = stronger opportunity. We flag anything above 8% as high confidence.

TUSG% Differential: Difference in true usage between teams. Large gaps (5%+) indicate significant mismatches in offensive responsibility.

PVR Differential: Efficiency advantage. A +5 PVR difference is substantial and often correlates with covering spreads.
