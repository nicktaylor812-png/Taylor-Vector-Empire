Why Position Doesn't Matter

Traditional NBA rankings are biased by position. A point guard's 25 PPG is valued differently than a center's 25 PPG. But True Usage Share (TUSG%) and Possession Value Rating (PVR) eliminate this bias.

TUSG% measures how much of the team's offense a player uses, regardless of position. PVR measures efficiency per possession. These metrics are era-adjusted and pace-neutral, allowing direct comparison between Magic Johnson and Stephen Curry, or Wilt Chamberlain and Giannis Antetokounmpo.

Longevity is measured by Seasons Played in the NBA. This captures sustained excellence and career durability, regardless of team success.
