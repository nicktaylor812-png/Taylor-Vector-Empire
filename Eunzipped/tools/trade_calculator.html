How Fit Score Works

The Fit Score measures team composition quality by analyzing player efficiency profiles:

High PVR + Low TUSG
Efficient role player - Perfect complementary piece (Score: 80-100)
High PVR + High TUSG
Star player - Can carry offense (Score: 70-90)
Low PVR + High TUSG
Ball-dominant inefficient - Bad fit (Score: 20-50)
Low PVR + Low TUSG
Limited role player - Okay fit (Score: 50-70)

Why this matters: Teams with complementary players (efficient role players + stars) perform better than teams with redundant usage patterns. Trading for high-efficiency players improves team fit score and overall performance.
