Understanding the Metrics

TUSG% (True Usage %)

TUSG% = (FGA + TOV + (FTA × 0.44)) / ((MP/48) × TeamPace) × 100

Measures what percentage of team possessions a player uses. Era-adjusted by team pace - higher pace means more possessions available, which affects the denominator.

PVR (Possession Value Rating)

PVR = [(PTS + (AST × Multiplier)) / (FGA + TOV + (0.44 × FTA) + AST) - 1.00] × 100

Measures efficiency per possession. PVR is NOT era-adjusted because it measures efficiency, not volume. A good shot is a good shot in any era.

Multiplier: AST/TOV ≤1.8 → 1.8x | AST/TOV >1.8 → 2.3x
